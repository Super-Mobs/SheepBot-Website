Moderation Commands

Command Alias Description Permission Level Ussage
ban None Bans a mentioned user. Currently does not require reason (no mod-log) 2 ban (user)
kick None Kicks a mentioned user. Currently does not require reason (no mod-log) 2 kick (user)
purge None This will remove X amount of messages sent in a channel, or by Y user. 2 purge (number 2-100) (user)
softban None Bans a mentioned user. Currently does not require reason (no mod-log) 2 softban (user)
